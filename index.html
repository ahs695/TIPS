M. A. Siddiqui

6 June 1939 – 19 January 2010  ·  Founder

Born in 1939, Munsif Ali Siddiqui devoted his entire life to education — with a special focus on Muslim education in Rajasthan. A triple post-graduate from Aligarh Muslim University, he retired as a Professor in Education and then moved to Jaipur, where he channelled his life's passion into establishing TIPS.

Our Story

A legacy built on a lifelong passion for learning

Before TIPS came to be, there was a man who believed education was the single greatest equaliser. Mr. Siddiqui began his career with Rajasthan's Statistical Department, but teaching was his true calling. He spent decades in classrooms and lecture halls before, in retirement, founding a school that gives every child in Jaipur access to quality English-medium education.

His vision lives on in every classroom. TIPS has been serving families for over 27 years — a co-educational, English-medium, recognised school built on warmth, discipline, and a deep love of learning.
